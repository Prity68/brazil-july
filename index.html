🔗 Shared itinerary sync

The itinerary is one shared plan for the whole group. To sync across all 6 iPhones in real time, set up a free JSONBin.io account:

  1. Create a free account at jsonbin.io
  2. Create a new Bin (the initial content can be {})
  3. Copy the Bin ID (looks like 64abc123...)
  4. Paste it below and share the same ID with your friends

Without a Bin ID, the itinerary saves locally on each device but won't sync across phones.

☀️ July-specific tips from data

What the data says about July in Brazil

  • 🌦 July is Brazil's dry season in the northeast — perfect for Lençóis Maranhenses lagoons
  • 🌊 Rio beaches are cooler (20–25°C) but surf is excellent. Bring a light layer for evenings
  • 🐆 Best time for Pantanal — dry season concentrates wildlife around waterholes
  • 🐬 Amazon water levels start falling — flooded forest canoe is still possible through July
  • 👥 Brazilian winter school holidays mean domestic crowds at beach destinations — book early
  • 🎭 No Carnival, but Salvador has Micareta (out-of-season Carnival) events in July some years
